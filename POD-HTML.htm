ALL CADETS:  If after the fall semester you are changing leadership tracks from ROTC to VPI, or from VPI to ROTC, you need to contact Lt Col Russell ASAP (druss135@vt.edu) to initiate the track change checklist process.  Delaying this transition causes administrative ripple effects, delays one’s academic and career development, and reflects poorly on one’s ability to manage and prioritize.

 

ALL CADETS: The Tailor Shop will be closed on Monday, 21 January as the University is officially closed for observance of Martin Luther King, Jr. Day.   For those cadets seeking rank insignia for the 2019 Spring semester or retrieve their Blue Blouse with amended rank, they can do so when the Tailor Shop opens at 0800, Tuesday, 22 January.
