A Cleaner World Uniform Storage

 

A Cleaner World will store your uniforms for the summer.  Uniforms will be cleaned, pressed and stored and be ready for pick up when you return in the fall.

 

Beginning THUR, May 9 (Reading Day), you may drop off your uniforms at 300 Stanger St., 120 Military Bldg. during the following times:

 

• MON – FRI   0745 – 0900 and 1630 – 1800

 

or -- at their local business address, 103 King Street (across from Wendy’s on South Main Street).  You may also drop them off in the Tailor Shop between the hours of 0800 and 1600.  Feel free to call A Cleaner World with any questions at 951-9506.

 

IMPORTANT:  If you will serve as cadre Fall 2019 or returning early in AUG, you must advise A Cleaner World upon drop off.
