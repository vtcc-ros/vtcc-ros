Ordnance Staff Interviews

 

The Regimental Ordnance Staff has openings for qualified cadets next year who wish to work in the Regimental Arms Room as armorers and become knowledgeable with the M1903A3 Springfield Rifle.  Current sophomore and freshmen cadets will be considered.  Applicants must be a freshman or sophomore cadet in good standing, have a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5, have successfully passed the applicable physical fitness test in their respective program, and have the approval of their Deputy Commandant.  Cadets on Academic, Military, or Honor Probation are ineligible.  Interested cadets will send an email to Cadet Captain Elijah Moats, the Regimental Armorer, at elim7@vt.edu and provide their current cumulative GPA, latest PT score, and date.  The window for submissions will close at 1700 Friday, 8 March 2019.  Mr. Moats will schedule interviews for finalists, and afterwards will submit recommendations to LTC Payne for final approval.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Performances in Moss Arts

 

The Regiment has received a very generous donation of tickets to programs at the Moss Arts Center this semester. 4 tickets are open to cadets for each performance and the sign-up sheets have been posted on the board next to the NCH laundry room. All are encouraged to sign up; however, since there are a limited number of spots, each cadet is limited to 1 performance. The tickets are for cadets only and may not be reserved for outside guests. Below are the dates and times of each performance:

 

Mar 16 1930 Danu St. Patrick’s Celebration

Mar 19 1930 Zadie Smith

Mar 23 1930 Postclassical Ensemble Deep River: The Art of the Spiritual

Mar 30 1930 David Rousseve/Reality Halfway to Dawn: The Strayhorn Project

Apr 1 1930 Terry Tempest Williams

Apr 7 1600 Australian Chamber Orchestra

Apr 12 1930 Turtle Island Quartet

Apr 26 1930 Fred Hersch, Kurt Elling, Kate McGarry Leave of Grass

May 11 1930 Pilobolus Shadowland 2: The New Adventure
