Cyber FastTrack

 

No experience needed! Cyber FastTrack is a free and unique three-stage program that will help you discover if you have what it takes to make a difference in cybersecurity.

 

This all-inclusive program, which starts in April 2019, will provide you with everything you need to build your knowledge of the foundations of cybersecurity. Once you have proven excellence in those foundations, you can move on to master forensics, intrusion detection, security operations, system and network penetration testing and application penetration testing. Designed by world-leaders in the field of cybersecurity, this program provides an unprecedented opportunity for you to fast track your career in cybersecurity!

 

https://www.cyber-fasttrack.org/

VTCC Blood Drive

 

The 2nd Virginia Tech Corps of Cadets Blood Drive is coming up! Mark your calendars for April 10th-12th and schedule an appointment with the Red Cross by visiting RedCrossBlood.org or calling 1-800 RED CROSS.

 

For this drive, Campus Cookies is donating a free cookie for each of the donors to have at the blood drive.

 

Donating blood can save up to 3 lives, and during the last Corps blood drive we had a total of 229 successful donations. Thank you for your dedication and support of this lifesaving cause. Those who volunteer their time, donate their blood, and spread awareness to the rest of campus truly embody That I May Serve.

 

If you have any questions about this upcoming blood drive, please contact C/Jung at caseyj1@vt.edu or (240) 566-2108.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Summer Job + Leadership Opportunity

 

RISING JUNIORS AND SENIORS/FIFTH YEARS: From 12 July to 5 August, work directly with CSM Willey and make money this summer while honing your leadership skills. You have the opportunity to serve as cadre for international students at a Massanutten Military Academy summer training camp. You will be leading students in military style leadership and outdoor adventure training. The pay is $250-$350 per week plus an additional stipend if you have Chinese language skills. All meals and lodging are included. There is an interest meeting Saturday, 30 MAR at 0930 in the PCR. Email CSM Willey at dwilley@vt.edu if you will be attending.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

New Student Orientation Staff

 

Looking for a rewarding job this summer?
