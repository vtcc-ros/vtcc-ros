IMPACT Peer Education Team

 

JOIN THE IMPACT PEER EDUCATION TEAM WITH HOKIE WELLNESS!

 

Opportunities for experience in: public speaking, 1:1 coaching, social marketing, and much more!

Now accepting applications for the 2019-2020 year.

 

Learn more and apply online by March 8th: www.hokiewellness.vt.edu/impact

Join a tight-knit team of students wanting to promote a positive campus community.

 

IMPACT (Initiating and Motivating a Positive Alcohol Culture Together) serves the Virginia Tech community through…

Outreach and Education: Do campus outreach, teach Party Positive classes, and facilitate the 21st Birthday Project! IMPACT teaches students about standard drinks, blood alcohol levels, and harm reduction strategies.

Advocacy: IMPACT advocates for a healthy Virginia Tech in a nonjudgmental way.

Meetings Every Wednesday, 5:30PM

Pay Per Hour: $9

 

Only current freshman, sophomore, and junior students will be considered.

 

Please contact C/Sinnott if you would like any more information or have questions at sliana15@vt.edu.

Performances in Moss Arts

Hokie Ambassadors

 

 

Hokie Ambassadors is an organization of passionate and dedicated students who love their school and volunteer their time to provide campus tours for prospective new students and their families. We are housed in Undergraduate Admissions and work alongside their efforts to facilitate recruiting new students and enhancing the visitor experience.
